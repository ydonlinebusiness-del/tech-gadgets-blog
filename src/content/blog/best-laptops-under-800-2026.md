## What to Look for in a Laptop Under $800 in 2026

**RAM:** 16 GB is the minimum worth buying in 2026. Laptops with 8 GB show meaningful slowdowns with more than 10 browser tabs open, running IDEs, or multitasking across apps. All five picks above include 16 GB.

**Storage type:** All SSD, no HDD. Any remaining budget laptop shipping with a hard disk drive in 2026 is not worth considering — the performance gap is so wide that a $500 laptop with an NVMe SSD outperforms a $700 laptop with an HDD for day-to-day tasks.

**Display resolution:** 1920×1200 (16:10) or higher. The 16:10 aspect ratio common in 2026 ultrabooks gives you more vertical space for documents and code compared to older 16:9 panels.

**Gaming needs:** If gaming matters, the minimum GPU worth buying is the RTX 4050 — integrated graphics (even Intel Arc and AMD Radeon) cannot run modern AAA games at acceptable settings.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best laptop under $800 in 2026?**

Yes — 16 GB handles everyday multitasking, software development, light video editing, and gaming comfortably in 2026. 32 GB becomes relevant if you run virtual machines, work with large datasets, or do professional 4K video production.
